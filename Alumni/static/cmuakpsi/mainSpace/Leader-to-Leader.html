Description



On August 31, 2013, brothers Sid Madan, Manny Garber, Olga Zubashko, Nat Eliason, and Nick Lam journeyed to State College, PA., some two hours away, to participate in the Leader to Leader Conference at Penn State University. The event was designed to create a forum for high level officers throughout the eastern region, and to discuss the below topics. In attendance, along with the Omega Psi chapter, were members from Xi Tau, Gamma Epsilon, Xi Pi, Nu Phi, Delta, and Iota Upsilon. Brothers reported learning a lot about how to keep older brothers engaged and deliberate and potential new members better, and recalled comical misunderstandings about our event "Lock-In".
