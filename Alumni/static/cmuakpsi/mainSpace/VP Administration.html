Overview

Vice President of Administration involves the management of a few key aspects within AKPsi. Foremost, the VPA is to the committee chairs what the President is to Eboard. It is his or her job to lead committee chair meetings, and ensure that committee chairs are contributing meaningfully to their positions and to the brotherhood. The VPA ensures the completion of successful events, as well as the coordination between the committee chairs and eboard.
Secondarily, the VPA manages the recording of ACR points and our relationship with nationals. It's his or her job to ensure we're on track for a high ACR score, and that we're meeting the requirements for national and regional attendance.
