Class Overview

Professor: Karim Kassam
Difficulty: A is possible if you do the work
Hours/Week: Less than 1, readings are pretty quick
Homework: No homework, but you want to do the readings because random quizzes on the readings make up 20% of your grade
Exams: 4 exams, 3 on the main topics, and 1 cumulative at the end. You can drop one exam.
