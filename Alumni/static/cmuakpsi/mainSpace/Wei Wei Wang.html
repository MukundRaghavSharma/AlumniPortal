In Memory...

Wei Wei Wang, a sophomore in economics, and a brother of Alpha Kappa Psi, passed away on Wednesday, April 12, 2006, from injuries she sustained in a car accident on Forbes Avenue.
Wei Wei was involved in many campus activities, including Kappa Phi Lambda, Students in Free Enterprise, and cheerleading. She worked in the Office of International Education, and was a teaching assistant for Advanced Chinese.
Wei Wei, for the year and half that you were in Alpha Kappa Psi, you brought us brightness. You brought us the best that you could, just like you did for everything. And Wei Wei, we would like to thank you.
  • Thank you for your passion and your kindness.
  • Thank you for bringing courage and enthusiasm to our brotherhood.
  • Thank you for encouraging us every step of the way.
  • Wei Wei, thank you for your dedication.
  • Thank you for saying ‘hi,’ the way you did.
  • Thank you for welcoming everyone.
  • Thank you for being so giving.
  • Thank you, Wei Wei, for always making the best out of every situation.
  • Thank you for teaching us to appreciate the color pink.
  • Thank you for your constant energy.
  • Thank you for always being there for us, no matter when or where.
  • Wei Wei, thank you for proving that there are people in this world who are pure and great in heart and mind.
  • Thank you for giving us 100% and more in all you did.
  • Thank you for your timeless smile.
  • Thank you, Wei Wei Wang, for being our brother. You will always be in our hearts as you kept us in yours.
With all our love,

Alpha Kappa Psi
