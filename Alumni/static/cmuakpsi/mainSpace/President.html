Position Duties

The main duties of President are:
  • Guide the activities and vision for the fraternity
  • Run all GBMs and Eboard meetings
  • Prepare the presentation/agenda for each GBM
  • Ensure Eboard members are on track and completing their goals and responsibilities
  • Decide and deliver AKPsi Awards at the end of each semester
