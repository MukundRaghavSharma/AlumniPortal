Overview

The Internship Panel is one of our more successful rush events. Brothers who have interned at "prestigious" companies are invited to give a 5-10 minute presentation on their experience, and how AKPsi helped them get there. This lets us establish our Ethos in a few ways: showing that we get good internships, showing that we're good at public speaking, showing we can make nice decks, and showing that AKPsi helped us along the way.
