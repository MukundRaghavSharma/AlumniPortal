About

FFS is one of the four finance focused under-graduate organizations on campus. The organization is primarily focused on the quantitative and research side of finance. Most of its members are interested in the quantitative analyst or risk analyst roles, with a few interested in sales & trading.

The club is structured with having weekly meetings comprising of a weekly discussion and projects. Every semester, the club sponsors several finance related projects, each led by two "project leaders". Projects range from research into negative swap spreads to stock valuation methods.

Outside of the weekly meetings and projects, the club hosts several speakers (usually related to quantitative finance) and a networking event at Citi's New York City office in the fall.
