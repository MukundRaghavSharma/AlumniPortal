Welcome to the CMU AKPsi Wiki!


The CMU AKPsi Wiki is an ongoing project by the Omega Psi chapter to catalog our collective knowledge of AKPsi, Professionalism, CMU, and life in general. In this wiki you'll find resources on numerous companies brothers have worked at, information on our alumni detailing their experiences in AKPsi and the professional world, descriptions of past pledge processes and the materials taught during them, and much more. We encourage you to add to this wiki in any way you can, as any knowledge you have to share will be valuable to future brothers. We only ask that you respect the formatting of similar pages to ensure consistency, and to not erase the work of anyone else without previously discussing it with them. Welcome to the AKPsi wiki, where everyone can share their water.

Getting Started

  • Find your name on the list of Active Brothers, and fill out your profile! Or, if you're an Alumni, go to the Alumni Profile Pages!
  • You could add your class experiences to the Class Reviews spreadsheet, or add more detail in the Class Summaries.
  • Have interview/internship experience? Why not add to our list of Company Profiles!
  • Have materials or memories related to pledging? Why not add to the Pledge Meetings, or Pledge Workshops pages?
  • Had or have a leadership position in AKPsi? Go ahead and add your knowledge/experience to the relevant AKPsi Leadership Page.
  • Have a particular professional skill you want to contribute? Add it to the Professional Development Database
