Description

This year, Lock-In was held on Sunday, September 1st, in Peter Wright Mckenna. The theme this year was "There's no place like home", designed to welcome brothers back to Carnegie Mellon and another semester of AKPsi, while at the same time acknowledge their experiences over the summer. Throughout the past several months, the Lock-In committee gathered photos and videos from the brotherhood of their internships, travels and alumni encounters to be shown throughout the event. As usual, the event focused on the values of our fraternity, dividing the time evenly amongst service, a professional event, and some games. The day concluded with some much needed preparation for the OLDP. Thanks our wonderful lock-in committee -- Nick Lam, Danning Wang, Lincoln He, Cynthia Hsia, and Sanika Natu.
