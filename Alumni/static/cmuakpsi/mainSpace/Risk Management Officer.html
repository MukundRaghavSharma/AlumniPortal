Overview

The Risk Management Officer (RMO) is a semester-long position on the Executive Board. The RMO will enforce all Risk Management policies as set by the national Alpha Kappa Psi organization; especially in regard to the Board of Directorsâ€™ Statement of Policy regarding Risk Management, the Constitution, and the Statutory Code. These policies shall be enforced at all Omega Psi chapter affiliated events (including but not limited to: meetings, banquets, social events, etc).

Furthermore, the RMO is responsible for creating an application for the Compliance Committee. Together, the RMO and Compliance Committee will review any event or circumstance that is not chapter-affiliated but that puts the good name of the chapter in jeopardy. The RMO/CC will utilize their best judgment and reasonable actions to stop any activity that they deem to be in violation of the aforementioned policies. The RMO is also responsible for leading any chapter-run investigation that the Executive Board deems necessary as a result of a violation of risk management policies.

In addition to these responsibilities, the RMO is expected to actively participate in Executive Board decisions. Attendance at all Executive Board meetings is required.
