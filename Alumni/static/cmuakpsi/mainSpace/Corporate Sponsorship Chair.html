Overview

The Corporate Sponsorship chair was designated in the Fall of 2011 to replace what was previously known as "Fundraising Chair." Coming in to the Fall 2011 semester, Hayden Tang (President), Brian Groudan (VP Membership) and Bernard Yuan (Fundraising) along with the help of other members of the fraternity succeeded in acquiring AKPsi's first official corporate sponsorship -- from Capital One. For the 2013-2014 school year, Alpha Kappa Psi's sponsors are Schell Games and Fino Consulting.

Past Position Events
In the Fall of 2013, Alpha Kappa Psi teamed up with Liberty Mutual to host a Case Interview Workshop. Liberty Mutual recruiters came and spoke to brothers about their product management positions, both full time and internship. Then, brothers broke up into groups and analyzed a case about potential insurance users and how to increase profitability for an insurance company. Afterwards, each group presented their analysis and proposed solutions for the case, and the recruiters gave their feedback.
