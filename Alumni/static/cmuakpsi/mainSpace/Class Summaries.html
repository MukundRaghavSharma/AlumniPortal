Overview

The class summaries page will allow brothers to discuss classes on a greater degree than the class reviews spreadsheet. These pages can be used as a resource to brothers who are currently taking the listed classes, or for brothers who want more in-depth descriptions of the classes they're debating on taking. Each page should include an overview of the topics covered, what the examinations are like, what the classes are like, what the professor(s) is(are) like, what the homework are like, what brothers have taken the class in the past, and any tips/tricks for excelling in the class.

When making a new page for a class, you should title the page "(Course #, Course Name)" i.e. "80-100 Intro to Philosophy" and follow the Class Summary Template, which will appear as an option for designing your page when you make a new one.
