Overview

The role of historian is to ensure the recording of our chapter's ongoing history, as well as to understand what has been done in the past and why. On eboard, historian should be the contributor most prepared to discuss how things were done in the past, how major decisions were made, and why Alpha Kappa Psi has done the things it has done. This requires strong connections with alumni, older brothers, and an interest in digging up old information that may have been buried or not recorded. This Wiki was created to facilitate not only the historian role, but each individual member's interest in learning more about where our fraternity has come from.

Historic Changes to the Position

Historian has always been somewhat fluid in what it seeks to accomplish. Some historians have focused on photo-documentation of events, some have focused on collecting large amounts of print or digital materials, and some have focused on dissemination of knowledge. Each holder of the position is entitled to interpret what it means to be "historian" in their own way.
